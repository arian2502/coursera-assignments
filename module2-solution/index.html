Our Characters

Mario!

Mario is a beloved video game character known for his iconic red cap, mustache, and overalls. He is a plumber turned hero in the Mushroom Kingdom, constantly rescuing Princess Peach from the clutches of Bowser. With his agility, jumping skills, and determination, Mario embarks on countless adventures, often accompanied by his brother Luigi and allies like Yoshi, battling enemies and overcoming obstacles.

Luiji!

Luigi is Mario's younger, taller, and slightly timid brother in the Super Mario series. Known for his distinctive green attire, Luigi often finds himself in Mario's shadow but proves his worth with his unique abilities. Despite his initial apprehension, he bravely joins his brother on countless adventures, displaying loyalty, humor, and occasional bursts of courage, making him a beloved character among fans.

Bowser!

Bowser, also known as King Koopa, is the primary antagonist in Nintendo's Super Mario series. He is a large, fire-breathing turtle who often kidnaps Princess Peach, prompting Mario and his allies to rescue her. Bowser is portrayed as power-hungry, cunning, and occasionally comical. Despite his villainous nature, he occasionally displays moments of vulnerability and has a complex relationship with Mario.
